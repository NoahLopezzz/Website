Hello, my name is Noah Lopez, I am a current member of the men’s soccer team here at UCR. We’ve been having a good season and making history, but there is a lot more to my life that has led me to be in the position that I’m in today. From a young age I played soccer and rose through the ranks. I wasn’t always a good player, and it took many years of work and determination.

Through my perseverance I received multiple offers to play Collegiate soccer, but I decided to commit to the University of California Riverside because it was close to home. I aspire to become a professional soccer player, but if that doesn’t work out I have my backup plan. I am also a biology major, which is very challenging, but I know that if I get through the 4 years of college, then I will give myself the opportunity to make a good living.

As a kid I always tried hard in school and took pride in getting good grades, and that mentality has transferred to college as well. My life is different from that of many, but I am extremely blessed to be in the position that I am in today
